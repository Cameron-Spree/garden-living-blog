The Composite Advantage

For decades, timber decking was the only real choice for a garden refresh. It looked great when first laid, but the upkeep was demanding. Today, more homeowners are shifting gears toward composite decking—a blend of wood fibers and recycled plastics that redefines outdoor living.

Expert Pro Tip

Tired of maintenance? Always leave an expansion gap between composite boards (typically 3-6mm) to allow for thermal expansion during the hot summer months. It prevents warping!
